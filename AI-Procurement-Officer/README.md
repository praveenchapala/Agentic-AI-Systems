# AI Procurement Officer

## Overview

AI Procurement Officer is an enterprise-grade Agentic AI system that automates vendor evaluation, procurement analysis, risk assessment, and supplier recommendations.

Instead of manually reviewing quotations, contracts, pricing, and delivery commitments, specialized AI agents collaborate to generate procurement insights and executive recommendations.

---

## Business Problem

Procurement teams spend significant time:

* Comparing vendor quotations
* Evaluating pricing structures
* Reviewing contract terms
* Assessing procurement risks
* Preparing executive recommendation reports

This process is often manual, time-consuming, and prone to inconsistency.

---

## Solution

The AI Procurement Officer automates procurement intelligence through a multi-agent architecture.

The system:

* Analyzes vendor quotations
* Compares pricing and delivery commitments
* Evaluates risks
* Reviews contract-related concerns
* Recommends the best vendor
* Generates executive procurement reports
